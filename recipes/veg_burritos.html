Veggie Burritos

Description

A veggie burrito is a flavorful, meat-free option featuring a soft flour tortilla stuffed with grilled vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and zucchini. It includes tender beans for protein, seasoned rice for heartiness, and slices of avocado for creaminess. Shredded cheese adds a melty texture, while fresh lettuce, tomatoes, and cilantro bring a crisp freshness. A squeeze of lime juice and a side of salsa, ranging from mild to spicy, enhance the flavors. This wholesome and satisfying dish combines smoky, savory, and tangy tastes in one convenient, rolled package.

ingredients

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Black beans
  • Rice
  • 1 onion(s), finely chopped
  • Tomatoes
  • shredded Cheese
  • Lime
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook Vegetables: In a skillet with olive oil, cook sliced bell peppers, onion, and zucchini with salt, pepper, and spices like cumin, garlic powder, and chili powder until tender.
  2. Warm Beans: Heat cooked black or pinto beans in a pot, seasoned with salt, pepper, and spices. Add water or broth if dry.
  3. Prepare Rice: Season pre-cooked rice with salt and lime juice.
  4. Assemble Burrito: On a large flour tortilla, layer rice, beans, grilled vegetables, shredded lettuce, diced tomato, chopped cilantro, avocado or guacamole, and shredded cheese.
  5. Roll Burrito: Fold in sides of tortilla, then roll from the bottom up, enclosing the fillings.
  6. Serve: Optionally cut in half and serve with salsa and a lime wedge.
